Mama JS Papa

Padithale inikkum

By mama

Megam polivathu unnale
En manadhu magilvadhum unnale
En uyirum udalum isaiyum kaviyum
unnal unnal peranbe...
Un manadhil irukum naaan thudipathu unidam edhardham
Un azhagiya punakai mattum en aaruyire vazhdhita uthavum suvasam
Naan kekka thudikum isaiyum unnaal....
En idhayathil thudikum thudipum unnaal
Naan thanimaiyil siripadhum unnaal...

By papa

Avano ennai katti anaidhu,
Mutham meda muthukalin parkal pathitu vituvan
Mayil iragugal pondra avan viralgalal ennai korthi,mayagi vaithu vituvan
Naano seru kolandhai yaai maari.....
En avan nenjil sainthu kollven
Avan idhaya thudipo ennnai thalatu paadi uranga vaithu vitum
En kanavaa..
